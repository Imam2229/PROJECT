About Blood Types

Knowing your blood type is important for donation. Here's a quick reference:

A+
A+ can donate to A+ and AB+
A-
A- can donate to A+, A-, AB+, AB-
B+
B+ can donate to B+ and AB+
B-
B- can donate to B+, B-, AB+, AB-
O+
O+ can donate to O+, A+, B+, AB+
O-
O- can donate to all blood types (universal donor)
AB+
AB+ can donate to AB+ (universal recipient)
AB-
AB- can donate to AB+ and AB-

Blood Donation Terms

By registering as a blood donor, you agree to the following terms:

  • You meet the eligibility requirements for blood donation in your region
  • All information provided is accurate and truthful
  • You understand that your contact information may be shared with patients in need
  • You consent to being contacted for blood donation requests
  • You will update your profile if your health status changes
Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Your personal information will only be used for blood donation purposes and will not be shared with third parties for marketing.
